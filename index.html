2024 Boston VIS summer School

Thank you to all who registered! We are excited to host you for two days of learning, networking, and exploring the latest in data visualization research. Below, you'll find the schedule and event details to help you plan your experience. We look forward to seeing you there!

Location

Venue: Shillman Hall, Room 320
115 Forsyth St., Boston, MA

Transportation: We highly recommend taking public transportation to the event. The closest T stop is Ruggles Station on the Orange Line or the Northeastern University stop on the Green Line.

Parking: If you prefer to drive, there is paid (metered) parking along Parker St., Hemenway St., and Fenway Dr. (some of which is unpaid). There is a parking garage available at Renaissance Park Garage, located at 835 Columbus Ave, Boston, MA 02120.
