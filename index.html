Data Visualization PhD Summer School

We are excited to announce that the inaugural Data Visualization PhD Summer School will be taking place Monday, August 8th and Tuesday, August 9th, hosted at Khoury College on the Northeastern University Boston campus!

Our 'home base' for the duration of the two-day conference will be Richards Hall ↪ Room 300 We will be eating food and beverage outside only, and insist that all participants must wear a mask when working indoors with other people. Students should take a few minutes before arriving to ask questions in Sli.do for our faculty Ask Me Anything (AMA), and fill out a hello slide in the presentation for Monday. Don't forget to bring a laptop for hands-on activities!
